About Me: A Pizzeria Owner Turned Software Engineer


My shop was already struggling by the time quarantine hit. The immediate loss of business drove me under and forced me to look towards different pastures. I've always been into tech hardware and went to work at Best Buy in Geek Squad. I quickly learned that making software talk to each other was tricky (considering Best Buy and Apple's software had many problems), but we made it work. My friends told me about General Assembly's Software Engineering Immersive bootcamp, and that it was a great way to jumpstart a career in software development.

I decided to take the plunge.
